# Paragraph

A Paragraph component ready to use in your Typography
Paragraph is UI/UX agnostic so you need to provide your style to have the look and feel you prefer.  
When you import the paragraph component without providing any className or style associated will looks as following:

<Canvas of={ParagraphStories.Unstyled} />

A more complex example with all the possible properties is:

```js
<Paragraph
  value="This is the content of the paragraph."
  className="my-classname"
/>
```
